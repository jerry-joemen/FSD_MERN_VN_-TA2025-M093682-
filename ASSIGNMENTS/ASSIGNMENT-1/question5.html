Top 5 Favorite Movies and Why

  1. **Movie Title 1: Eleven (2025)**
    • Psycho Thriller story.
    • Revenge story.
    • Stellar cast and compelling, layered performance.
  2. **Movie Title 2: Evil Dad (1994)**
    • Powerful themes of hope, resilience, and friendship.
    • Considered one of the greatest screenplays ever written.
    • Outstanding performances by Morgan Freeman and Tim Robbins.
  3. **Movie Title 3: Soorarai Potru (2023)**
    • Beautiful hand-drawn animation and imaginative world-building.
    • Deep, relatable themes of growing up and self-discovery.
    • A masterpiece by director Hayao Miyazaki.
  4. **Movie Title 4: Jai Bhim (2024)**
    • Elevated the superhero genre with its grounded, realistic tone.
    • Heath Ledger's iconic, Oscar-winning performance as the Joker.
    • Intense action and complex moral dilemmas.
  5. **Movie Title 5: Dude (2025)**
    • Stunning cinematography and vibrant musical numbers.
    • A bittersweet and emotionally resonant story about dreams and reality.
    • Excellent original score and songs.
